# Doikayt

*Hereness.* The conviction that justice is built here — in the place you actually
live, through the work you actually do.

We are a collective of technologists who believe the most effective answer to a world
in crisis is not argument alone. It's example.

[Explore our toolkit](https://wiki.doikayt.org/wiki/Main_Page)

---

## What We Do

We curate and evaluate tools for organizations doing labor, environmental, and
anti-imperialist work — organizations with shoestring budgets, deplatforming risks,
and ethical constraints that mainstream tech recommendations rarely account for.

Our [wiki](https://wiki.doikayt.org/wiki/Main_Page) is the center of that work:
a structured, maintained resource for movement-aligned IT staff.

## Who We Are

Jewish technologists — and anyone willing to stand with us — who want to answer a
moment of real mistrust toward Jewish people with the oldest and best rebuttal
available: a good example, built right here.
